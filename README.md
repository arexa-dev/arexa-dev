## About Arexa

Arexa is a global XR and immersive technology company focused on creating next-generation digital experiences through Augmented Reality, WebAR, CGI, Virtual Try-On systems, and interactive spatial technologies.

We help brands, agencies, startups, enterprises, and creators transform traditional content into immersive experiences that drive engagement, retention, and conversion.

Our work spans:

- Augmented Reality Experiences
- Snapchat Lens Development
- WebAR Platforms
- AI + XR Integrations
- Virtual Try-On Systems
- High-Fidelity CGI Production
- Interactive 3D Experiences
- Immersive Marketing Campaigns
- Spatial Computing Solutions
